We are a private Catholic coeducational liberal arts college founded in 1928 by the Sisters of St. Joseph of Springfield, Massachusetts. Elms College is located in Chicopee, Massachusetts, and grants associate’s, bachelor’s, strong - master’s, and doctoral degrees.

Tertiary Heading

The RN-BS degree completion program at Elms College is an accelerated and affordable 15-month program designed to advance your nursing career. Delivered in a cohort model with individualized advising, you’ll progress through the program with peers in a supportive group.

Elms RN-BS nursing courses emphasize nationally recognized competencies for nurses and health professionals. The RN-BS program of study integrates a strong liberal arts and sciences curriculum with the nursing specialization. Elms College nursing students develop the communication skills and the critical thinking ability that prepares them to drive change in healthcare and make important contributions to patient safety and quality care.
